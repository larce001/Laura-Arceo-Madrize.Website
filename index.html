My name is Laura Vanesa Arceo Madriz. I am a third year Biochemistry major with an emphasis in medical science. I decided on this major after deciding I wanted to work in healthcare. As many other student I came into college as a premed student. After much research I decided that nursing is the path I actually want to take. I am specifically intrested in becoming a pediatric nurse or a midwife nurse. I love doing community work and although I mostly volunteer with school related organizations, these experinces have helped me realize I love working with children It has also put me in positions where parents leave their trust in me which is also very important to understand you are not only working and affecting the children's life but also their families. I am very proud of my goals and representing my community in the medical field some day. I come from the city of East Palo Alto a small forgotten city in Silicon Valley. It is a super diverse neighborhood which historically was home to mostly people of color. East Palo Alto is full of history. For a long time the city was looked down upon. It gained a repuation and people from outside judged and set very low expecation for the children of the city. Today I am proud of beating all the odds and surpassing expections. I am proud to be an example and role model for all the kids I volunteer with.
